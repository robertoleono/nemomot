About NEMOMOT

An independent imprint.
For everyone who wants to live well.

NEMOMOT was founded by Roberto León — an entrepreneur who learned most of what matters the hard way. Not in a classroom. Not in a conference room. In the real friction of building things, losing things, and figuring out what the system never bothered to explain.

The imprint exists because the books Roberto needed at critical moments in his life didn't exist in the right form — clear enough to act on, honest enough to trust, beautiful enough to want to finish. So he built the publishing house he would have wanted as a reader.

NEMOMOT is for people who work hard, think seriously, and want a life that is free, sane, and worth living. We publish straight — no false promises, no academic fog, no motivational noise. Just the clearest version of the most important ideas we know.

What we stand for
01
Truth before comfort
We publish books that tell you what is true, not what is easy to hear. Our readers are adults. We treat them that way.
02
Ideas grounded in evidence
Every NEMOMOT title is built on research, real experience, or both. We don't publish opinions dressed as facts.
03
A community, not an audience
Our readers don't just consume — they belong. NEMOMOT is a place for people who believe that living well is a project worth taking seriously.
