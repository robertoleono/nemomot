About NEMOMOT

An independent imprint.
For everyone who wants to live well.

NEMOMOT is an independent publishing imprint built for people who feel, at some level, that they were never given the full picture — about money, about the mind, about the systems shaping their lives.

We don't publish books to fill shelves. We publish them because the ideas inside are too important to leave buried in academic language, hidden behind paywalls, or diluted into generic self-help. Every NEMOMOT title is built to be read in full, acted on immediately, and kept for years.

Independent. Unaffiliated. Global.

What we stand for
01
Truth before comfort
We publish books that tell you what is true, not what is easy to hear. Our readers are adults. We treat them that way.
02
Ideas grounded in evidence
Every NEMOMOT title is built on research, real experience, or both. We don't publish opinions dressed as facts.
03
A community, not an audience
Our readers don't just consume — they belong. NEMOMOT is a place for people who believe that living well is a project worth taking seriously.
