The Protocol

6 practices.
10 minutes each.

Not heroic. Not demanding. Specific enough to work at the level where the problem actually lives — in the daily habit of delegating thought.

01
The Deliberate Recall

Rebuilding the retrieval pathways that search engines and AI have made optional — without making the practice feel like homework.

10 min · Daily

02
The Unassisted Draft

Writing one thing per day without AI assistance — not to produce better prose, but to keep the generative circuits active.

10 min · Daily

03
The Idle Hour

Reclaiming the mind-wandering state that smartphones have colonized — and that neuroscience identifies as the source of insight and creativity.

10 min · Daily

04
The Slow Read

Reading at a pace that demands inference and connection — the cognitive work that skimming and AI summaries eliminate entirely.

10 min · Daily

05
The Reasoned Position

Forming and defending a judgment without outsourcing the reasoning — training the evaluative capacity that AI use atrophies fastest.

10 min · Daily

06
The Deep Attention Block

Holding a single complex problem in mind for a sustained period — the capacity that the notification environment has most systematically destroyed.

10 min · Daily

The Science

"Users who relied on ChatGPT showed significantly lower neural connectivity than those who completed tasks independently — and the effect persisted even when AI access was removed."

MIT MEDIA LAB · "Your Brain on ChatGPT" · 2025

Duration of the study. The cognitive effects were measurable after the very first session and compounded over time.
