The Protocol

6 practices.
10 minutes each. Every day.

No app. No subscription. No requirement to quit AI. Just 6 targeted habits that protect the cognitive capacities that AI cannot recover for you — starting week one.

01
The Friction Threshold
Define the minimum cognitive effort required before delegating to AI. Trains the pre-frontal cortex to engage before outsourcing.
10 min · Day 1
02
The Ownership Draft
Write the first 200 words of any piece before using AI. Activates neural ownership and deep encoding of the idea.
10 min · Day 3
03
The Recall Window
24 hours after any AI-assisted work, summarize it from memory. Builds the hippocampal circuits that AI atrophies.
10 min · Day 5
04
The Judgment Audit
Weekly review of the 3 decisions where you trusted AI without questioning it. Rebuilds critical evaluation circuits.
10 min · Day 7
05
The Original Argument
Once a week, form and defend a position on something important without consulting any AI. Protects independent reasoning.
10 min · Day 10
06
The Deep Session
One 45-minute block per week of sustained thinking on a single problem, no AI, no search. Recovers sustained attention capacity.
45 min · Day 14
