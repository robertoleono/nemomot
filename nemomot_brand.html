An imprint built on ideas that matter

NEMOMOT was founded on the belief that the most important conversations of our time are not happening in conference rooms or on social media. They're happening in books — when a writer with years of research and a real point of view meets a reader willing to be challenged.

We are selective by design. Every title we publish is chosen because it has something genuinely new to say — grounded in evidence, written with care, and built to last longer than a news cycle.

What we stand for
01
Research over opinion
Every NEMOMOT title is grounded in data, clinical evidence, or rigorous scholarship — not just a compelling argument.
02
Writing that earns its place
We believe good ideas deserve beautiful prose. We work with writers who care as much about the sentence as the thesis.
03
Readers as partners
Our community isn't an afterthought. Early readers shape how our books reach the world — and how the world responds.
